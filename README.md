## Why Proper AC Installation Matters

A lot of homeowners think any air conditioner will do the job. The truth? Even a top-tier unit performs poorly when it's incorrectly sized or improperly installed. An undersized AC runs constantly and still leaves you sweating. An oversized one short-cycles — flipping on and off repeatedly — which skyrockets your hydro bill and wears out the equipment fast.

Getting the installation right matters as much as picking the right brand. Our technicians are certified and experienced in:

- Load calculation to determine exact cooling capacity for your home
- Proper refrigerant line handling and charging
- Electrical connections and disconnect box setup
- Condensate drainage installation
- Thermostat wiring and calibration
- Final system testing and performance checks

When we finish an installation, your system should cool your home evenly, run efficiently, and last for years — not months.

## Signs It's Time for a New Air Conditioner

Not sure if you need a replacement or a repair? Here are some clear signals that a new installation makes more sense than patching up an old system:

- Your AC is 10–15 years old or more
- Repair costs are adding up and exceed half the cost of a new unit
- Your energy bills keep rising despite no change in usage habits
- The system uses R-22 refrigerant (now phased out in Canada — parts are scarce and expensive)
- Uneven cooling or persistent hot rooms despite technician visits
- Frequent breakdowns, especially at the start of cooling season
- The compressor has failed (usually a sign the whole unit should be replaced)

If two or more of these apply, a new installation almost always makes better financial sense in the long run.

Installation costs vary depending on the type and size of the system, brand, and any additional work needed (ductwork modifications, electrical upgrades). We offer free in-home estimates so you get an accurate, no-obligation quote before committing.

**How long does AC installation take?**
Most standard central air conditioner installations are completed in one day — typically 4 to 8 hours. Ductless mini-split installs can sometimes be done in 3 to 4 hours, depending on the number of zones.

**Do I need a permit for AC installation in Mississauga?**
In most cases, a permit is required for HVAC installations in Ontario. We handle the permit process on your behalf, so you don't need to worry about navigating city requirements on your own.

**Can you install AC in a home without existing ductwork?**
Yes. Ductless mini-split systems are the ideal solution for homes without ducts. They're efficient, flexible, and require minimal invasive installation work.

**What size air conditioner do I need for my home?**
Size is calculated based on your home's square footage, insulation quality, ceiling height, window orientation, and local climate. This is called a Manual J load calculation. Our technicians perform this assessment during the free in-home estimate.

**What brands do you install?**

**What maintenance does a new AC system need?**
